Boar Jerky

Description

Boar jerky is made with only two ingredients: boar meat and honey. The ratio of boar meat and honey are 1:1.

Ingredients

  • Cauldron
  • Boar Meat (raw)
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Marinate combined ingredients in desired mixture for 12 hours.
  2. Heat cauldron.
  3. Combine 1 part uncooked boar meat to 1 part honey into cauldron.
  4. Cook for 3 hours on low heat.
  5. Remove from cauldron when meat is thoroughly dried.
  6. The jerky should be dry to the touch, and chewy but still tender.
