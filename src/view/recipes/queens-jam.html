Queens Jam

Description

Queens jam is made with two ingredients: Raspberries and blueberries. The ratio of the two ingredients are 4:3.

Ingredients

  • Cauldron
  • Raspberries
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Heat cauldron.
  2. Combine 4 parts raspberries to 3 parts blueberries into cauldron.
  3. Gently mash berries in cauldron until desired texture is reached.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes on low heat.
  5. Remove from cauldron.
  6. Refrigerate jam for increased shelf life, and enjoy!
