Deer Stew

Description

Deer stew is made with three ingredients: Cooked deer meat, carrot, and blueberries. The ratio of all three ingredients are 1:1:1.

Ingredients

  • Cauldron
  • Deer Meat (cooked)
  • Carrot
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Heat cauldron.
  2. Combine equal parts of cooked deer meat, carrot, and blueberries into cauldron.
  3. Cook for 6 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Remove from cauldron when carrot is soft, or when desired texture is achieved.
  5. Let stew cool for 5 minutes before consuming, and enjoy!
