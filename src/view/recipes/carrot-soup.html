Carrot Soup

Description

Carrot soup is made with only two ingredients: carrots and mushrooms. The ratio of carrots and mushrooms are 3:1.

Ingredients

  • Cauldron
  • Mushroom
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Heat cauldron
  2. Combine 1 part mushroom to 3 part carrot into cauldron
  3. Cook for 4 minutes on high heat
  4. Pour from cauldron to prevent over-cooking
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes and enjoy
